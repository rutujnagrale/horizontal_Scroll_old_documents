ImageTextEval

In this project, the goal is to develop a system that can enhance and clear text from old historical images using image processing techniques implemented with OpenCV (Open Source Computer Vision Library) in Python. Historical documents often suffer from degradation over time, including faded text, stains, and noise, which can make the text difficult to read.

What is Text Evauation

Text evaluation involves the systematic assessment and analysis of written content to gauge its quality, relevance, and effectiveness for a given purpose or audience. This process encompasses various aspects, including evaluating the accuracy, clarity, coherence, and grammatical correctness of the text to determine if it meets desired standards. Additionally, text evaluation involves analyzing the content to assess its depth, coverage of topics, and informativeness. Sentiment analysis may also be part of text evaluation, helping to identify the emotional tone or sentiment conveyed by the text.
